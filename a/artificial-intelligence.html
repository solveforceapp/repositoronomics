Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Definition

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems. It encompasses learning, reasoning, problem-solving, perception, and language understanding, enabling systems to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence.

Application

AI is used in a wide range of applications, including natural language processing, robotics, predictive analytics, cybersecurity, and customer service. In telecom and IT, AI enhances automation, network optimization, and threat detection.
