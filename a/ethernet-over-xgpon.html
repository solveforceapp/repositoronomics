Ethernet over XG-PON

Definition

Ethernet over XG-PON (10 Gigabit-capable Passive Optical Network) is a technology that delivers ultra-high-speed Ethernet services over advanced fiber optic networks.

Application

Used by ISPs and large enterprises to provide gigabit and multi-gigabit broadband access for demanding applications and future-proof connectivity.
