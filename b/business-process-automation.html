Business Process Automation

Definition

Business Process Automation (BPA) is the use of technology to execute recurring tasks or processes in a business where manual effort can be replaced. BPA streamlines operations, reduces errors, and increases efficiency.

Application

BPA is applied in areas such as customer service, billing, HR, and supply chain management. Automation tools and software robots help organizations save time, cut costs, and improve service quality.
