DDoS (Distributed Denial of Service)

Definition

A DDoS attack is a malicious attempt to disrupt the normal traffic of a targeted server, service, or network by overwhelming it with a flood of internet traffic from multiple sources.

Application

DDoS mitigation is critical for telecom, cloud, and enterprise networks to ensure service availability and protect against cyberattacks.
