Ethernet over SONET/SDH

Definition

Ethernet over SONET/SDH is a technology that enables Ethernet frames to be transported over Synchronous Optical Network (SONET) or Synchronous Digital Hierarchy (SDH) infrastructure, combining Ethernet flexibility with optical network reliability.

Application

Used by carriers and enterprises to connect distant sites with high-speed, reliable, and scalable network services.
