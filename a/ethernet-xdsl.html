Ethernet xDSL

Definition

Ethernet xDSL refers to Ethernet services delivered over various types of Digital Subscriber Line (DSL) technologies, such as ADSL, VDSL, and SHDSL, using existing telephone infrastructure.

Application

Provides broadband connectivity in areas lacking fiber or cable, supporting business and residential internet access.
