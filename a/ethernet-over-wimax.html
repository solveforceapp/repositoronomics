Ethernet over WiMAX

Definition

Ethernet over WiMAX is a technology that delivers Ethernet services using WiMAX (Worldwide Interoperability for Microwave Access) wireless broadband networks, enabling high-speed connectivity in areas without wired infrastructure.

Application

Used by ISPs and enterprises to provide broadband access in rural, remote, or underserved regions where fiber or cable is unavailable.
