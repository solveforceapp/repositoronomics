Black Screen

Definition

A black screen is a display issue where the monitor shows no image, often indicating a hardware failure, software crash, or connection problem. It is a common troubleshooting scenario in computers and electronic devices.

Application

Black screen issues are diagnosed by checking hardware connections, graphics cards, and software settings. IT professionals use systematic troubleshooting to identify and resolve the root cause, restoring normal operation for users.
