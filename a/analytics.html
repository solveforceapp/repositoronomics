Analytics

Definition

Analytics refers to the systematic computational analysis of data or statistics. In IT and telecom, analytics involves examining data sets to draw conclusions, identify patterns, and support decision-making. It encompasses descriptive, predictive, and prescriptive analytics.

Application

Analytics is used in business intelligence, network monitoring, cybersecurity, and customer experience optimization. Organizations leverage analytics to improve operations, detect anomalies, forecast trends, and gain competitive advantage.
