Business Continuity

Definition

Business Continuity refers to the planning and processes that ensure critical business operations can continue during and after a disaster or disruption. It involves risk assessment, contingency planning, and recovery strategies.

Application

Organizations implement business continuity plans to minimize downtime, protect assets, and maintain customer trust. This includes backup systems, disaster recovery, and regular testing of response procedures in IT and telecom environments.
