Cybersecurity

Definition

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks, unauthorized access, and damage. It encompasses technologies, processes, and policies designed to safeguard information and ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of digital assets.

Application

Cybersecurity is essential for businesses, governments, and individuals to defend against threats such as malware, phishing, ransomware, and data breaches. It involves risk assessment, security controls, incident response, and ongoing monitoring to protect critical infrastructure and sensitive information.
