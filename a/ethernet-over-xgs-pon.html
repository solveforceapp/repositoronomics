Ethernet over XGS-PON

Definition

Ethernet over XGS-PON (10 Gigabit-capable Symmetrical Passive Optical Network) is a fiber optic technology that delivers symmetrical gigabit Ethernet services for high-performance networking.

Application

Used by ISPs, data centers, and enterprises to provide ultra-fast, reliable broadband for cloud, streaming, and business-critical applications.
