Ethernet over WDM

Definition

Ethernet over WDM (Wavelength Division Multiplexing) is a technology that transmits multiple Ethernet signals over a single optical fiber by using different wavelengths, maximizing bandwidth and efficiency.

Application

Used in metropolitan and long-haul networks to deliver high-capacity, scalable Ethernet services for carriers and large enterprises.
