Architecture

Definition

Architecture in IT and telecom refers to the conceptual structure and logical organization of a system, network, or application. It defines the components, their relationships, and the principles guiding their design and evolution.

Application

Architectural frameworks are used to design scalable, secure, and maintainable systems. Examples include network architecture, software architecture, and enterprise architecture, each providing blueprints for building and managing complex environments.
