Ethernet over MPLS

Definition

Ethernet over MPLS (Multiprotocol Label Switching) is a technology that enables Ethernet frames to be transported across MPLS networks, combining the simplicity of Ethernet with the scalability and reliability of MPLS.

Application

Used by service providers to deliver flexible, high-performance Ethernet services over wide area networks (WANs).
