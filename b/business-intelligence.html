Business Intelligence

Definition

Business Intelligence (BI) refers to technologies, processes, and practices for collecting, integrating, analyzing, and presenting business data. BI helps organizations make informed decisions by providing actionable insights from raw data.

Application

BI tools are used for reporting, data visualization, and performance management. Enterprises leverage BI to identify trends, optimize operations, and gain a competitive edge in the market.
