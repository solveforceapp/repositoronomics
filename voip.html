Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is VoIP?

VoIP (Voice over Internet Protocol) is a technology that allows you to make voice calls using a broadband internet connection instead of a regular phone line.

What is UCaaS?

UCaaS (Unified Communications as a Service) is a cloud-based platform that delivers integrated communication services such as voice, video, messaging, and collaboration tools.

Can I keep my existing phone numbers?

Yes, number porting is supported for most carriers and regions.

Is VoIP Integrator secure?

Yes, all communications are encrypted and monitored for security threats.

How long does setup take?

Most deployments can be completed in a matter of hours, depending on complexity.

Glossary

  • PBX: Private Branch Exchange, a private telephone network used within a company.
  • SIP: Session Initiation Protocol, used for signaling in VoIP communications.
  • IVR: Interactive Voice Response, an automated telephony system.
  • QoS: Quality of Service, prioritizing network traffic for optimal performance.
  • UC: Unified Communications, the integration of various communication methods.
