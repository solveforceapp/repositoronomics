Ethernet VPN

Definition

Ethernet VPN (EVPN) is a network technology that uses Ethernet as the underlying transport for virtual private networks, enabling flexible, scalable, and efficient multi-site connectivity.

Application

EVPN is used by service providers and enterprises to deliver advanced Layer 2 and Layer 3 VPN services over MPLS or VXLAN networks.
