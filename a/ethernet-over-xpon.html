Ethernet over xPON

Definition

Ethernet over xPON (Passive Optical Network) refers to delivering Ethernet services over various PON technologies, such as GPON, XG-PON, and XGS-PON, for high-speed fiber broadband.

Application

Used by ISPs and enterprises to provide scalable, high-capacity broadband access for residential, business, and campus networks.
