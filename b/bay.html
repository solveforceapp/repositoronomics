Bay

Definition

A bay is a slot or compartment in a computer case designed to hold drives, such as hard drives, SSDs, or optical drives. Bays provide a standardized space for installing and securing storage devices or other hardware components.

Application

Bays are essential for expanding a computer's storage capacity or adding new devices. They are used in desktops, servers, and some laptops, allowing users to upgrade or replace drives as needed for performance or capacity.
