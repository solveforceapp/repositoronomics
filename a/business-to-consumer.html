Business-to-Consumer (B2C)

Definition

Business-to-Consumer (B2C) refers to transactions or relationships between businesses and individual consumers. B2C is common in retail, e-commerce, and service industries, where companies sell products or services directly to end users.

Application

B2C platforms include online stores, telecom service providers, and digital content distributors. They focus on user experience, marketing, and customer support to attract and retain consumers.
