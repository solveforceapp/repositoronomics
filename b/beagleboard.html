BeagleBoard

Definition

BeagleBoard is a low-power, single-board computer designed for embedded applications, development, and education. It features an ARM processor and a range of I/O options, making it suitable for prototyping and custom hardware projects.

Application

BeagleBoard is used in robotics, IoT devices, and multimedia systems. Its open hardware design and community support make it popular for learning, rapid prototyping, and deploying embedded solutions in both academic and industrial settings.
