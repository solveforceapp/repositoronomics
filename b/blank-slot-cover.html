Blank Slot Cover

Definition

A blank slot cover is a metal or plastic plate used to cover unused expansion slots on a computer case. It helps maintain proper airflow, prevent dust buildup, and protect internal components from electromagnetic interference.

Application

Blank slot covers are used in desktops and servers to keep the interior clean and cool. They are important for system maintenance, ensuring optimal performance and longevity of hardware by reducing dust and maintaining airflow patterns.
