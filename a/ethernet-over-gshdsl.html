Ethernet over G.SHDSL

Definition

Ethernet over G.SHDSL (Single-pair High-speed Digital Subscriber Line) is a technology that provides symmetrical Ethernet connectivity over copper telephone lines, supporting business-class broadband services.

Application

Ideal for businesses needing reliable, symmetrical bandwidth for data, voice, and video applications in areas without fiber.
