Cellular

Definition

Cellular refers to wireless communication systems that divide geographic areas into cells, each served by a base station, enabling mobile devices to connect and communicate over large distances.

Application

Cellular technology underpins mobile phone networks (2G, 3G, 4G, 5G), supporting voice, text, and data services for billions of users worldwide, and is essential for IoT, emergency response, and mobile broadband.
