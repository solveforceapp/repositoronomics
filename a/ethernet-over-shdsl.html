Ethernet over SHDSL

Definition

Ethernet over SHDSL (Symmetric High-speed Digital Subscriber Line) is a technology that provides symmetrical Ethernet connectivity over copper telephone lines, supporting equal upload and download speeds.

Application

Ideal for businesses needing reliable, symmetrical bandwidth for applications like VoIP, video conferencing, and cloud services.
