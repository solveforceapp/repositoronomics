Cyberspace

Definition

Cyberspace refers to the virtual environment created by interconnected computer networks, including the internet, intranets, and other digital communication systems. It is a conceptual space where digital interactions, data exchange, and online activities occur.

Application

Cyberspace is the domain for online communication, commerce, social networking, and cyber operations. It is central to modern business, government, and personal activities, and is a focus for cybersecurity and digital policy development.
