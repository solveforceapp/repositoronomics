Ethernet over zDSL

Definition

Ethernet over zDSL refers to delivering Ethernet services over a range of DSL technologies, including ADSL, VDSL, and SHDSL, using existing copper telephone lines for broadband access.

Application

Used by ISPs to provide cost-effective broadband connectivity in areas where fiber or cable is not available.
