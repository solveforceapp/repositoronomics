Toll Free Numbers & Call Routing

Toll Free Services provide businesses with national and international toll free numbers, advanced call routing, and analytics to enhance customer engagement and support. Make your business more accessible and professional with a dedicated toll free presence.

What are Toll Free Services?

Toll Free Services enable organizations to offer customers a free and easy way to contact them. Calls to toll free numbers are billed to the business, not the caller, making it ideal for customer service, sales, and support hotlines.

Key Features

  • National and international toll free numbers (800, 888, 877, etc.)
  • Advanced call routing and IVR
  • Time-of-day and location-based routing
  • Disaster recovery and failover options
  • Real-time call analytics and reporting
  • Integration with VoIP and PBX systems
  • Number porting and vanity numbers
  • 24/7 support and management

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a toll free number?

A toll free number is a telephone number billed to the business, allowing callers to reach you at no charge.

Can I port my existing number?

Yes, most toll free numbers can be ported from your current provider.

Are international toll free numbers available?

Yes, we offer international toll free numbers in many countries.

Can I get a vanity number?

Yes, custom vanity numbers (e.g., 1-800-MY-BRAND) are available.

How quickly can I set up a new number?

Most numbers can be provisioned within 1 business day.
