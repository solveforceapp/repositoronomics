Block Diagram

Definition

A block diagram is a schematic representation of a system, showing the major components or functions as blocks connected by lines that indicate relationships or data flow. It is used to visualize the structure and operation of hardware or software systems.

Application

Block diagrams are used in engineering, electronics, and IT to design, analyze, and communicate system architectures. They simplify complex systems, making it easier to understand, troubleshoot, and document hardware and software designs.
