Beta Testing

Definition

Beta testing is a phase in software development where a product is released to a limited audience outside the development team for real-world use and feedback. It helps identify bugs, usability issues, and areas for improvement before the final release.

Application

Beta testing is used by software companies to gather feedback from actual users, ensuring the product meets user needs and works in diverse environments. It is a critical step for quality assurance and customer satisfaction.
