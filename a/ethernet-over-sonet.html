Ethernet over SONET

Definition

Ethernet over SONET (Synchronous Optical Network) is a technology that enables Ethernet frames to be transported over high-speed optical networks, combining the flexibility of Ethernet with the reliability of SONET.

Application

Used by carriers and large enterprises to interconnect geographically dispersed networks with high reliability and bandwidth.
