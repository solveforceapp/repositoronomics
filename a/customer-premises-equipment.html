Customer Premises Equipment (CPE)

Definition

Customer Premises Equipment (CPE) refers to telecommunications equipment located at the customer's premises, such as modems, routers, and phones, that connects to the service provider's network.

Application

CPE is essential for delivering broadband, voice, and video services, enabling end users to access and manage network connectivity and services.
