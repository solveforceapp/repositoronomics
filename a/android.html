Android

Definition

Android is an open-source operating system based on the Linux kernel, designed primarily for touchscreen mobile devices such as smartphones and tablets. Developed by Google, Android powers a vast ecosystem of devices and applications.

Application

Android is the dominant mobile OS globally, used by device manufacturers, app developers, and telecom providers. It supports a wide range of applications, from communication and productivity to entertainment and IoT integration.
