Blu-ray Disc

Definition

A Blu-ray Disc (BD) is an optical disc storage medium designed to supersede the DVD format. It offers higher storage capacity and supports high-definition video and data storage, using a blue-violet laser for reading and writing data.

Application

Blu-ray Discs are used for distributing movies, games, and software in high definition. They are also used for data backup and archiving, providing reliable, long-term storage for personal and professional use.
