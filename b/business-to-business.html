Business-to-Business (B2B)

Definition

Business-to-Business (B2B) refers to commercial transactions or relationships between two businesses, rather than between a business and individual consumers. B2B is common in IT, telecom, manufacturing, and wholesale sectors.

Application

B2B platforms and services enable companies to buy, sell, and collaborate efficiently. Examples include cloud service providers, telecom carriers, and enterprise software vendors serving other businesses.
