Agile

Definition

Agile is a project management and software development methodology that emphasizes iterative progress, collaboration, and flexibility. Agile approaches break projects into small, manageable increments called sprints, allowing teams to adapt to changing requirements and deliver value quickly.

Application

Agile is widely used in software development, IT, and telecom projects to improve productivity, enhance communication, and accelerate delivery. Frameworks like Scrum and Kanban are popular Agile implementations, enabling teams to respond rapidly to customer feedback and market changes.
