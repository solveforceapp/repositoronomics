Captive Portal

Definition

A captive portal is a web page that users are automatically directed to when connecting to a public Wi-Fi network, typically requiring authentication or acceptance of terms before granting internet access.

Application

Captive portals are used in hotels, airports, cafes, and enterprise guest networks to manage user access, collect user data, enforce policies, and provide branding or advertising before allowing internet connectivity.
